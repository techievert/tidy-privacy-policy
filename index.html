Privacy Policy for Tidy

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

Tidy ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application ("Tidy App").

When you use Tidy, we may process the following information:

2. How We Use Your Data

Your photos and videos are temporarily processed by our AI backend to generate cleaning checklists. We do not share your media publicly, and it is used only to provide the app's core functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party AI services to generate checklists. These services only process the data you provide for checklist generation and do not retain it beyond processing.

4. Data Retention

Your media files and checklist data are stored locally on your device. We do not store media files permanently on our servers.

5. User Choices

You may delete any saved checklist or media from the app at any time using the app's delete functionality.
